# Scanner Rebrand — 36 naming concepts (2026-07-01)

Product truth to name against: one high-conviction stock pick, delivered before the open plays out, built from volume/structure evidence. Institutional feel, not memecoin.

## Tier 1 — Signal & foresight
1. Bellwether — leading indicator + the opening bell; double meaning, institutional
2. Foresight — plain, confident
3. Argus — the hundred-eyed watchman; sees the whole tape
4. Sentinel — always watching, fires rarely and deliberately
5. Vigil — quiet watchfulness, premium minimalism
6. Overwatch — command-center energy
7. Watchtower — market surveillance height

## Tier 2 — The morning / the open
8. First Light — the pick arrives at first light, before 9:45
9. Daybreak — same idea, warmer
10. Opening Print — trader-native (the first print of the day)
11. NineThirty — owns the moment itself
12. The Bell — short, iconic
13. Lead-Off — first at-bat of the day
14. Early Line — sportsbook "opening line" energy

## Tier 3 — Precision instruments
15. Meridian — navigational precision, premium sound
16. Sextant — old-world instrument for fixing position
17. Astrolabe — heritage instrument, distinctive
18. Aperture — focus on one thing sharply
19. Prism — splits the whole market into one clear signal
20. Caliper — measurement, exactness
21. Vector — direction + magnitude, quant-clean

## Tier 4 — Speed & the hunt
22. Peregrine — fastest animal on earth; dives on one target
23. Kestrel — hovers, waits, strikes once
24. Osprey — precision fisher, one clean strike
25. Harrier — low, fast sweep of the field
26. Flashpoint — the exact moment a move ignites
27. Ignition — where momentum starts

## Tier 5 — Navigation & stars
28. Polaris — the one fixed point to steer by
29. True North — conviction, single direction
30. Zenith — the day's highest point
31. Ephemeris — the daily astronomical table; "your daily table of moves"
32. Almanac — daily edge, heritage feel

## Tier 6 — Brand-cohesive (Theta family)
33. Theta Prime — flagship designation
34. Theta One — "the one pick"
35. Theta Meridian / Theta Bellwether — lockups
36. Prime Mover — physics + first mover of the session

## Recommendation: **Bellwether** (lockup: "Bellwether — by Theta Algos")
Why: (1) real meaning that matches the product exactly — a bellwether IS the thing that signals where the rest will go; (2) the bell pun lands on the opening bell without being cute; (3) institutional register (Bloomberg would name something this) yet not a TradingView clone; (4) ownable as a sub-brand with room for tiers (Bellwether Pro, Bellwether Futures).
Runners-up: First Light (best emotional fit for a pre-9:45 morning pick; softer authority), Peregrine (best if the brand leans speed/precision), Meridian (safest premium-neutral).
Check trademark/domain before committing (bellwether is used by some funds; as a feature sub-brand inside Theta Algos the risk is low, but verify).
